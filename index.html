WHY NANOFORGE

A more programmable approach to nanobody discovery

Traditional antibody discovery can be slow, costly and dependent on animal immunisation. NanoForge is developing a modular workflow designed to improve speed, control and downstream candidate quality.

01

Animal-free discovery

Synthetic libraries provide controlled, scalable starting points without relying on animal immunisation.

02

Faster iteration

Computational prioritisation and experimental screening support efficient design-build-test cycles.

03

Developability-aware

Expression, stability, specificity and manufacturability can be considered earlier in discovery.

OUR PLATFORM

Integrated nanobody discovery technology

The platform is designed to connect computational design with synthetic biology and experimental validation in a closed-loop discovery workflow.

01

AI-guided design

Computational methods prioritise sequences with promising binding, stability and developability characteristics.

02

Synthetic libraries

Diverse, target-focused libraries provide an animal-free foundation for nanobody discovery.

03

High-throughput screening

Scalable workflows identify and rank promising binders against relevant biological targets.

04

Candidate optimisation

Iterative selection and testing aim to improve affinity, specificity, expression and stability.

OUR SERVICES

Protein and antibody development support

NanoForge is also building practical protein science capabilities to support early research, validation and assay development.

Recombinant protein expression

Expression strategy, optimisation and production support across bacterial and mammalian systems.

Protein and antibody purification

Workflow development using affinity, ion-exchange and size-exclusion chromatography.

Protein characterisation

Analytical support including SDS-PAGE, Western blotting, concentration measurement and quality assessment.

Custom assay development

Design and optimisation of fit-for-purpose biochemical, binding and screening assays.

ABOUT NANOFORGE

Scientific expertise driving biologics innovation

NanoForge Biologics is being developed by scientists with experience in protein engineering, recombinant protein production, antibody purification, assay development, structural modelling and synthetic biology.

Our long-term mission is to make high-quality nanobody discovery faster, more predictable and more accessible for therapeutic, diagnostic, imaging and research applications.

OUR MISSION
